📘 How to Play

OMO is a fun twist on Tic-Tac-Toe! The goal is to spell out W-O-W on the grid.

  • 🎯 Players take turns placing their marks (W or O) on the grid.
  • ✨ If a player successfully spells “WOW” in any direction (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal), they score a point.
  • 🔁 When a player scores, they get an extra turn.
  • 🏆 The game continues until the grid is full, and the player with the highest score wins!

Play against friends online by creating or joining a room, or play solo against the AI at different difficulties.
